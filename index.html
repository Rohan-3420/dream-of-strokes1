Meet the Artists

Led by Rohan Shahzad, a 16-year-old artist from Pakistan who began painting in 2021. Despite his young age, he has created over 300 unique, handmade paintings and won 2nd place in the Junior Artist competition in Pakistan (2022).

Together with three other talented artists, Dream of Strokes brings you authentic, handcrafted artwork that tells a story and captures emotion in every brushstroke.
